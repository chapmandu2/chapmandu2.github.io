Consulting & Freelance

Phil Chapman

I was previously the Lead Scientist for Bioinformatics and Data Science in the Drug Discovery Unit at the Cancer Research UK Manchster Institute. I used this experience to provide independent bioinformatics and drug discovery data science consultancy. My scientific specialisms are preclinical drug discovery and cancer genetics, and my technical specialisms are in R and data science. But take a look at my blog posts and GitHub projects to see if I might be able to help you!

Note that due to the nature of my current role at Egress Software Technologies I am only able to work with non-profit and academic groups.
