Key Bioinformatics Databases

Phil Chapman

  • cBioPortal
    • Understand genetic landscape of different tumour types
  • ICGC Portal
    • International Cancer Genomics Consortium Portal - Understand genetic landscape of different tumour types
  • Broad CCLE
    • Understand specifics of cell lines - affy, mutations
    • Can search for cell lines with a given mutation, can also check BAM file
  • Cosmic CLP
    • Exome sequencing of cell lines
  • Sanger Genomics of Drug Sensitivity
    • Eurofins-like cell line sensitivity to drugs
  • PharmacoDB
    • Collation of Sanger GDS dataset plus a number of others
    • PharmacoGx Bioconductor package for further analysis
  • Oncomine
    • Commercial collection of gene expression datasets - useful for understanding whether a gene is up/down regulated in a cancer type
